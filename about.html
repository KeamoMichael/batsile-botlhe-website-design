About Us

About Batsile Botlhe

Built on excellence,
driven by community.

Established in 2024, Batsile Botlhe Project Development is a proudly black male-owned and managed construction company, registered with the CIPC, NHBRC, and the Construction Industry Development Board.

Born from a drive to participate meaningfully in South Africa's BEE structures, Batsile Botlhe has never looked back in its quest to deliver exceptional service — building a reputation for outstanding expertise across all service requirements.

We have earned acknowledgement for undertaking complex ventures, embracing modernization, and implementing new and effective ways of serving clients — all in order to make a difference for our clients, employees, and community.

We deliver quality service at competitive prices, with full recognition of each client's requirements and a commitment to cost-effective, high-level service delivery that still values the personal touch.
